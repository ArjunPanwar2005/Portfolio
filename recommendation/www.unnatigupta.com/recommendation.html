“You give them recommendations. You throw different angles at them where, hopefully, they can get something out of it.”

— Mike Butcher

You may have an impressive resume and an outstanding list of professional achievements. But if you also have a recommendation for your outstanding contribution in some domain you may have a distinct advantage over other applicants in job search or reserving a spot in an educational institution of world repute.

A recommendation letter describe your approach to your future goals , as well as your philosophy as a student. It provides evidence that your learning outcomes have been successful & also documents that you have made efforts to improve as a student or professional over the years. Fortunately, I had privilege to receive appreciation for a few of my assignments which I undertook during my school days to satisfy my quench to contribute to humanity, exploring my mentoring abilities & honing my skills in diverse fields and also learning from veteran in their respective fields.
